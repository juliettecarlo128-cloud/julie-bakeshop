FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about ordering, location, and deposits.

What do you bake?

I can bake anything by request. Some of my specialties are Banana Bread, Tres Leches, Carrot Cake, Cinnamon Rolls, and Cookies.

Where are you located?

I'm located in Hialeah, FL. I do not have a physical store, but I offer delivery and pickup for your convenience. There is a delivery fee depending on distance.

Do you require deposits?

Deposits are required in order to place an order. It is $20 to secure your baked goods, and it goes towards the total payment. Deposits are non-refundable.
